Our Foundation

Why We Exist: Our Core Mission

Our main objective is to empower young people through Agriculture by providing quality chicks and imparting skills on how to conserve the environment by distributing quality tree seedlings. This ensures they master the process of nurturing a tree from its seedling state to maturity. Our formation was motivated by the urgent need to address the growing environmental challenges and food insecurity exacerbated by climate change. Recognizing the untapped potential of youth to contribute meaningfully, we aim to empower them through education, resources, and hands-on initiatives. By nurturing a generation of environmentally conscious and agriculturally skilled individuals, we aspire to build a resilient society prepared to tackle the climate crisis head-on.

Action & Results

Our Current Impact and Initiatives

Reforestation Drives

We actively distribute free tree seedlings to schools and communities. This vital initiative combats deforestation and instills a deep sense of responsibility in young people to care for their local environment and climate.

Poultry & Farming Skills

We provide quality chicks and comprehensive training on proper poultry care to students. This program empowers youth and communities to practice sustainable farming, enhancing livelihoods and food security.

Educational Workshops

Through engaging educational workshops and motivational campaigns, our organization inspires youth and communities to take proactive steps in mitigating and adapting to the effects of climate change.

Transforming Futures

Empowering Youth Through Agriculture

Practical Skills Training

We equip young people with hands-on agricultural skills in poultry farming, crop cultivation, and environmental conservation. These practical skills create immediate employment opportunities and foster entrepreneurship in the agricultural sector.

Economic Independence

By providing quality chicks, tree seedlings, and training, we enable youth to start their own agribusinesses. This approach directly addresses unemployment by creating sustainable income streams and reducing dependency on formal employment.

Climate Resilience

We train youth in climate-smart agriculture techniques, preparing them to adapt to changing environmental conditions. This knowledge makes them valuable assets in building community resilience against climate change impacts.

Join the Movement: From Job Seekers to Job Creators

Every young person we train becomes a catalyst for change in their community. Through agriculture, we're not just planting trees and raising chickens, we're cultivating a generation of green entrepreneurs who will drive Kenya's sustainable development.
